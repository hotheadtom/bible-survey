The people were not constantly travelling after they left Egypt. 
People were settled down when God gave them laws.
This is the book of travel.
People starts travelling when the cloud rises up; stops when the cloud comes down.
In Exodus people travelled till Sinai.
In Numbers, people travel from Sinai to Moab

36 chapters - 4th book of Moses

Book Name
    2 censes - when people start travelling from Sinai and when they arrive at Moab; so the book is called Numbers

Book Division
    1 - 14 - Old generation - Sinai to Kadesh-barnea
    15 - 20 - Old generation wanders in desert and dies off; new generation comes up
    21 - 36 - New generation travels from Kadesh to Moab

God instructs before journey how to proceed and tells them after each journey what to do next
