40 chapters - Moses

1 - 18 : Exodus - from bondage to freedom
19 - 24 : Law - to show God's holiness, to teach man his responsibility, to show the standard of righteousness, to expose & reveal the sinfulness of man (Gal 3: 24)
25 - 40 : Tabernacle - to teach people to worship God. shows God's wisdom, fellowship with God, great status for God's people
